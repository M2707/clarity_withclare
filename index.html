About Clare

A teacher still
on her own journey

Clare Garvey is a yoga teacher, Oxygen Advantage instructor, and HeartMath mentor based in Omagh, Northern Ireland. She teaches because she believes in what she teaches — not from a place of having it all figured out, but from walking the path herself every single day.

Her classes are gentle, grounding, and always real. She’ll never make you feel like you need to be more flexible, more spiritual, or more “ready.” You just have to show up.

— Clare
Yoga Teacher Oxygen Advantage HeartMath Mentor Breathwork Nervous System

Why Clarity
with Clare?

Because too many brilliant women walk around convinced they’re not flexible enough, spiritual enough, or “yoga enough.” Clare’s classes exist to change that.

  • Gentle Hatha — suitable for absolute beginners
  • Small classes in welcoming, intimate spaces
  • Breathwork and nervous system tools you can use every day
  • A community that genuinely has your back
  • Pricing that reflects where you actually are right now
